# 图像提示词库 v1

> 用途：沉淀可复用的提示词骨架、关键词、negative prompt 模块，以及不同图像母题下的高频表达。  
> 状态：v1，持续追加。

---

## 一、使用原则

提示词库不是“句子仓库”，而是：
- 可复用骨架
- 高频有效词
- 易错项对照表
- 可拼装模块

一句话：

> **不要背 prompt，要学会拼 prompt。**

---

## 二、五层提示词骨架

任何参考图反推，都优先按这五层组织：

1. **Subject（主体）**
2. **Space / Composition（空间与构图）**
3. **Anchor / Terminal Object（锚点物 / 终点物）**
4. **Environment / Megastructure（环境 / 巨构）**
5. **Mood / Style（情绪 / 风格）**

---

## 三、可复用 Prompt 模板

### 模板 A｜世界观定调图
```text
[shot / aspect ratio], [tiny subject], [vast environment], [distant anchor object], [colossal environmental structure], [space relationship], [lighting contrast], [mood], [style reference], [rendering style]
```

### 模板 B｜叙事切口图
```text
[shot], [single character action], [danger or event swallowing the environment], [limited visibility or strong atmosphere], [story moment], [mood], [style], [composition note]
```

### 模板 C｜巨构压迫图
```text
[tiny human figure], [colossal isolated megastructure], [not skyline], [dwarfing the subject], [negative space], [environment dominates frame], [solemn / monumental atmosphere]
```

## 四、关键词模块

### 1. 人物尺度关系
可用：
- tiny lone figure
- extremely small human figure
- human figure as a scale anchor
- dwarfing the human figure

慎用：
- heroic figure
- dramatic protagonist pose
- centered character portrait

---

### 2. 超宽空间感
可用：
- extreme wide cinematic shot
- ultra wide shot
- huge horizontal space
- strong negative space
- the environment dominating the frame
- centered horizon

慎用：
- close-up
- portrait framing
- square composition

---

### 3. 远方终点物
可用：
- single distant pale sun
- low on the far horizon
- remote white disc
- distant pale celestial disc
- not oversized
- not dominating the frame

慎用：
- gigantic glowing sphere
- huge orb
- giant central sun

---

### 4. 巨构 / 文明残骸
可用：
- enormous abandoned futuristic megastructure
- colossal isolated industrial monolith
- dead machine-ruins
- collapsed industrial city core
- the dead remains of a space station
- not a city skyline
- sacred pillars of a collapsed civilization

慎用：
- skyline
- dense buildings
- futuristic city
- clustered architecture
- background city

---

### 5. 情绪母题
可用：
- quiet end-of-the-world atmosphere
- solemn pilgrimage feeling
- industrial mythology
- epic but calm
- monumental
- desolate
- terminal journey
- quiet doom

慎用：
- intense action scene
- explosive chaos
- dramatic battle

---

## 五、negative prompt 模块

### 1. 防双圆 / 多天体
```text
two suns, two moons, multiple glowing spheres, extra celestial bodies, duplicated light sources, oversized sun, giant central orb, foreground glowing ball
```

### 2. 防城市化 / skyline 化
```text
city skyline, dense buildings, urban background, clustered architecture, modern city silhouette, futuristic city skyline
```

### 3. 防对称摆件 / 小土坡
```text
symmetrical composition, mirrored ruins, two matching structures, mounds, hills, rock piles, small ruins
```

### 4. 防海报化 / 方图化
```text
square composition, portrait framing, centered oversized object, close-up, crowded foreground
```

### 5. 防跑偏成别的题材
```text
battle scene, explosions, vehicles, monsters, neon cyberpunk, fantasy castle, steampunk, anime, cartoon, readable text, logo, watermark
```

## 六、案例模块（当前收录）

### 案例 1｜文明末日朝圣图
#### 有效方向
- 超宽画幅
- 人物极小
- 终点物远小
- 巨构为偏置独体残骸
- 环境压倒主体

#### 易错方向
- 圆太大
- 双圆
- 建筑变 skyline
- 建筑变左右小土坡
- 1:1 海报感

#### 当前可用 prompt 核心
```text
extreme wide cinematic shot of a tiny lone figure in a vast red desert, facing a single distant pale sun low on the far horizon, an enormous abandoned futuristic megastructure dominating the left side of the frame, like the dead remains of a space station or collapsed industrial city, colossal black monolithic architecture dwarfing the human figure, red-orange haze, huge horizontal space, strong negative space, quiet end-of-the-world atmosphere, solemn and monumental, highly atmospheric sci-fi concept art
```

## 七、使用建议

### 建议 1
先从模板出发，再拼关键词，不要每次从空白写 prompt。

### 建议 2
每轮只改 1~2 个变量：
- 比例
- 终点物
- 巨构阅读
- 主体大小
- 空场多少

### 建议 3
一旦发现模型总往某个错误方向偷懒，立刻把它写进 negative prompt 模块。

---

## 八、一句话总结

> **好的提示词库，不是收藏漂亮句子，而是能帮助你稳定守住图像里最关键的关系。**
